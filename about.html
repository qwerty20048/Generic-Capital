About the firm

Generic Capital Management Limited is a premier business consulting firm committed to excellence, integrity and client success. Founded with a vision to guide enterprises through transformative growth, our team brings decades of industry expertise across finance, operations, business incubation, strategic development and assurance. Since commencement of business in 2005, Generic Capital has served as a corporate platform for providing strategic consulting services to private and public sector clients to help them thrive in rapidly evolving markets. Our expert teams deliver customized solutions that drive growth, efficiency and innovation.

Business Model

Our business model is to seek close partnership with clients to identify and understand their exact needs and then design specific action plans to meet those needs in a holistic, sustainable and mutually beneficial manner. Each assignment is considered peculiar in its own way as we do not adopt the consulting strategy of one-size-fits-all. Generic Capital strives to build internal capacity within the client organization to enable changes introduced to be owned and internalized.
